# Chinese Learning Reading Materials

[English](README.md) | [中文](README_CN.md)

---

This project is designed for non-native Chinese speakers to learn Chinese primarily through reading Chinese web literature.

## Project Introduction

This project is based on the philosophy that "reading is the most effective way to learn any language." It provides easy, interesting, and engaging reading materials for Chinese learners - Chinese web literature. These materials are graded by difficulty, from simple to difficult, suitable for learners at different levels.

## Getting Started

Before starting to read, it's recommended to test your Chinese vocabulary size first:
https://www.arealme.com/chinese-vocabulary-size-test/cn/

## Vocabulary Grading Standards

According to the "International Chinese Education Chinese Proficiency Level Standard" (GF 0025-2021) issued by the Ministry of Education and the National Language Commission, the Chinese vocabulary grading system is as follows:

| Level | Vocabulary | Cumulative Vocabulary | Corresponding HSK Level |
| ----- | --------- | --------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Elementary Level 1 | 500 words | 500 words | HSK 1-2 |
| Elementary Level 2 | 772 words | 1272 words | HSK 2-3 |
| Elementary Level 3 | 973 words | 2245 words | HSK 3-4 |
| Intermediate Level 4 | 1000 words | 3245 words | HSK 4-5 |
| Intermediate Level 5 | 1071 words | 4316 words | HSK 5-6 |
| Intermediate Level 6 | 1140 words | 5456 words | HSK 6 |
| Advanced Level 7-9 | 5636 words | 11092 words | HSK 7-9 |

  * "Jingwei Fills the Sea"
  * "The Old Man at the Frontier Lost His Horse"
  * "Tian Ji's Horse Race"
